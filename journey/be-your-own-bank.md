- Seed phrases: the 12 or 24 words behind everything
- Wallet types: hot, cold, hardware, multisig — when each makes sense
- Receiving your first transaction
- Verifying with a watch-only wallet
- Backup hygiene: paper, steel, geographic separation
- What NOT to do (the common ways people lose coins)

Generate a seed. Receive a few sats. Verify them. Sign a message. That's the proof you've done it.
