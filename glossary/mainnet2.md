Periodically, discussions or jokes surface about an imaginary “Mainnet2” as if Bitcoin might get a full refresh. In reality, Bitcoin evolves through soft forks (occasionally hard forks in contentious scenarios) without launching a separate chain that entirely replaces mainnet. The continuous backward-compatible approach has kept the single mainnet intact since 2009. ‘Mainnet2’ stands as a tongue-in-cheek concept about an alternate dimension where Bitcoin restarts, removing legacy constraints or addressing deeply ingrained issues. But no serious proposal to wipe the slate clean or break from the existing chain is on the table.
