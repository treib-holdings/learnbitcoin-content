‘GZen’ is like a crypto-minded take on ‘stoic patience.’ Instead of panicking over every dip or euphoria spike, you maintain a zen-like detachment, focusing on Bitcoin’s long-term value proposition. It’s a playful cultural reference among hodlers who endure volatility by embracing an almost meditative acceptance.
While not an official strategy, GZen resonates with the idea of dollar-cost averaging and ignoring FUD-laden headlines. By transcending daily price drama, adherents believe they can avoid poor decisions driven by market hysteria. It’s essentially telling fellow Bitcoiners, “Relax, zoom out, and trust the protocol’s core strengths.”
