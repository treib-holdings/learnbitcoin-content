Alt season is like that surprise moment when the side dish at dinner steals the spotlight from the main course. During these cycles, altcoins-everything from Ethereum to obscure tokens-tend to surge in price faster than Bitcoin. Traders who time it well can see massive percentage gains.
However, alt seasons can be volatile and unpredictable. While Bitcoin remains the most established cryptocurrency, altcoins often attract speculators with promises of novel technologies or use cases. It can feel like a whirlwind carnival of possibilities, but caution is advised. Not every altcoin has strong fundamentals; some ride hype waves without delivering real innovation.
