Falling on Bitcoin's 'birthday' (the Genesis block date), Proof of Keys is a community-led event reminding users about true ownership. By withdrawing coins to personal wallets, individuals confirm that exchanges or custodians are actually holding the BTC they claim to. It's akin to a stress test: if many try and fail to withdraw, it could reveal insolvency or fractional reserve practices. The campaign underscores the principle that if you don't control your keys, you rely on someone else's solvency and good faith-a direct clash with Bitcoin's self-sovereign ethos.
