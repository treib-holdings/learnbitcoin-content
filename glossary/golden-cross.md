In trading, the golden cross typically occurs when the 50-day moving average crosses above the 200-day moving average, signaling potential upward momentum. Many see it as a buy indicator, although it's far from guaranteed. Within Bitcoin markets, such crossovers can coincide with or predict price rallies, especially if sentiment and fundamentals align. However, as with any TA pattern, it's best used cautiously alongside broader market analysis, since crypto volatility can invalidate signals quickly.
