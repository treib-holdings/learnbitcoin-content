A 'Minsky Moment' is when asset prices suddenly plunge, triggered by a realization that speculative debt or leverage is unsustainable. Although it originates in traditional finance, Bitcoin commentators sometimes invoke the phrase when BTC's price experiences a sharp correction after a period of euphoria or leveraged trading. While not strictly a Bitcoin phenomenon, the crypto market's volatility and margin-friendly trading can amplify the effect. Recognizing such moments helps traders anticipate or react to large-scale liquidations and capitulations.
