On cryptocurrency exchanges worldwide, buyers and sellers post orders, and executed trades reveal what price BTC can command in real time. This constant push and pull-shaped by global sentiment, macro trends, supply/demand dynamics-defines Bitcoin's price discovery. Volume, liquidity, and market structure heavily influence how accurately price reflects real demand. When markets are thin or dominated by a few large players, price volatility can spike. Conversely, diverse, liquid markets produce smoother, more resilient price discovery over time.
