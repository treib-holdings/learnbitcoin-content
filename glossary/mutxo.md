Occasionally, the term 'MUtxo' surfaces in developer chatter, denoting a situation where multiple outputs exist under one wallet domain. It might appear in advanced scripts or CoinJoin contexts, or simply as a playful way to say 'this wallet has a lot of UTXOs to manage.' Not a widely formalized term, it remains more of a casual shorthand. Splitting or merging MUtxos has significant privacy and fee implications, so it's a common discussion point among those optimizing or analyzing on-chain usage.
