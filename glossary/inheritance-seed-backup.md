In Bitcoin, there's no 'official' executor or customer support if you pass away. An inheritance plan prevents your BTC from being lost forever by detailing how heirs can retrieve the seed phrase or multisig keys. This may include locked documents stored with a lawyer or instructions on navigating hardware wallets. More sophisticated setups use time locks, dead man's switches, or external trustees. Balancing usability (so heirs aren't locked out) with security (avoiding premature access or theft) requires careful design. Consider testing the plan periodically to ensure it works smoothly when needed.
