Bitcoin's base layer remains slow and limited in throughput. Layer-2 solutions address these constraints by shifting frequent transactions off-chain or onto side networks. Lightning channels bundle multiple payments before one final on-chain settlement, slashing fees and confirmation times. Sidechains like Liquid or RSK also let users peg BTC to a separate chain for faster blocks or extra functions (e.g., confidential transactions). While trust models vary, the fundamental goal is to keep mainnet lean and secure while offering advanced features or higher transaction capacity off-chain.
