In Bitcoin's earliest era, any CPU could contribute meaningful hashing power, but now mining profitability demands specialized ASICs. Smartphones are drastically underpowered by comparison- the energy cost would exceed any possible BTC earned. Some altcoins with CPU-friendly algorithms might be mineable on phones, but typically it's a gimmick. Attempts to mine on Android apps or iPhone are not cost-effective and can overheat or damage devices. Hence, in modern Bitcoin mining, phone mining is more novelty than viable strategy.
