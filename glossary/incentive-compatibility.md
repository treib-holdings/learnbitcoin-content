In economics, an incentive-compatible system ensures that rational participants benefit from following the rules rather than cheating. Bitcoin miners devote hashing power to finding valid blocks because it yields block rewards and transaction fees. If they try to mine invalid blocks, the network rejects them, wasting resources. Similarly, nodes enforce consensus rules because that’s how they guarantee they hold valid BTC. This synergy of self-interest and collective benefit underpins Bitcoin’s resilience: it’s typically more profitable (and rational) to play by the rules than to attack or deviate.
