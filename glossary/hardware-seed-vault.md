A hardware seed vault is like a digital fortress purpose-built to keep your private keys or seed phrases out of reach from hackers. It could be a tamper-resistant device, a specialized computer with no internet access, or a hardware security module (HSM). By generating and storing seed data offline, you significantly reduce the risk of malware or remote attacks. The key idea is that the seed never sees a connected environment, minimizing avenues for theft. This approach is popular with large holders, institutions, and anyone who values airtight security for long-term storage.
