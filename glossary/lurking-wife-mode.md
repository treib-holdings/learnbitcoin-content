Bitcoin Core developers have a sense of humor. In older commits, a config named 'lurkings Wife Mode' or 'HideBalance' was joked about, letting you obscure wallet balance from prying eyes-possibly an inquisitive spouse or coworker. While it's not an official feature in current releases, references persist as a fun bit of developer culture. It underlines the open-source tradition of Easter eggs or comedic commits that occasionally surface in serious software. For real privacy, other methods (like locking the wallet or using a watch-only mode) are more robust solutions than a tongue-in-cheek toggle.
