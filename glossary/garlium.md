Garlium emerged when altcoin enthusiasts cloned Electrum's codebase for Garlicoin, a minor cryptocurrency project. While interesting from a developmental standpoint, it never gained broad traction or significant user adoption. It highlights how Electrum's open-source architecture can be forked for different chains, but for Bitcoin users, Garlium is mainly a footnote in the annals of altcoin experiments.
